Snapchat Review Analysis

  • Description:The aim of snapchat review analysis is to provide insight into the public’s opinions, emotions and feelings towards a particular event. The objective is to extract and analyze the sentiments,reviews and emotions expressed in snaps on the snapchat.It is the process of using natural language processing techniques to extract and analyze the sentiment, opinion, and emotions expressed in Snaps. Sentiment analysis is a type of study which enables the researchers or product developers to analyze users comments, opinions or expressions towards certain products or services . In this We are going to be looking at the sentiments, in other words, opinion or belief, of all 10k reviews and see if each review is Positive, Negative, or Neutral.

  • Language Used:Python

  • Software Used:Jupyter notebook and libraries like VaderSentiment,Wordcloud,TextBlob,NTLK and so on to perform review analysis on snapchat data.
